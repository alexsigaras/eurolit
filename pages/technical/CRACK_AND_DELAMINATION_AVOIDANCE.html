Crack and delamination avoidance

Cracked and delaminated floors are not acceptable and can even be considered dangerous in modern warehouse and logistic facilities. Cracks and delaminated areas will only continue to deteriorate over time causing vibrations on the high speed moving trucks resulting in, increased wear of the trucks wheels, malfunctions in their electronic components, production and accumulation of dust on merchandise and personnel safety hazards, thus increasing maintenance cost and downtime.

Eurolit, taking into consideration the local climatic conditions, is able to offer expert advice on crack and delamination avoidance methods.

With the implementation of Dramix steel fibers in conjunction with local reinforcement schemes, appropriate concrete mix design , surface treatment techniques, pours dimension properties, joint depth, joint spacing properties and compatible curing agents all appropriate measures are taken to minimize crack and delamination occurrence.
