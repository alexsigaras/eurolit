About us

Eurolit has been at the forefront of design, construction and renovation of industrial floor technology for more than three decades, constantly questioning old notions regarding efficiency, materials, procedures and execution methods.

We specialize in consulting, project management and construction of Steel Fiber Reinforced Concrete, Magnesite Screeds and Diamond Polished Concrete.

Alongside our technical expertise, we offer a turnkey solution to our clients, supporting them from the initial design and planning to installation of the floor and surveying.
