Diamond Polished Concrete

Superfloor Terrazzo

Suited mostly for areas with flatness and/or esthetic issues. Eurolit uses diamond technology to deeply grind and polish the floor according to the client's needs without the use of any chemicals, dust and odor emission. During the process, aggregates are exposed revealing the natural beauty hidden inside concrete. The new floor profile is much smoother, crack free, does not produce any dust, retains its monolithic and its slip resistant properties, is immune to wheel marks and requires no cleaning agents to maintain its shiny appearance severely minimizing maintenance cost and time.

Up Grade

Suited for areas suffering from deeply penetrated dirt or poor esthetics, Eurolit uses diamond technology to finely grind and polish the floor without the use of any chemicals, dust and odor emission. During the process even accumulated dirt is removed and the surface appearance looks even better than when brand new.

The new floor profile retains its surface hardness and monolithic properties, does not produce any dust, retains its slip resistant properties, is immune to wheel marks and requires no cleaning agents to maintain its shiny appearance severely minimizing maintenance cost and time.
