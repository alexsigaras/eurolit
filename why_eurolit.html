Why Eurolit

George Sigaras

Founder

The notion that an industrial floor needs only to be sturdy and relatively smooth is by far outdated and does not comply with the fast changing environment surrounding the manufacturing and the logistics business. Demand for high efficiency, low cost, safe and future proof facilities is in rise.

Such facilities though, require a holistic approach and deeper understanding regarding design, specifications, materials, local environment and execution methods which are key to the success of any project.

Here at Eurolit, our main focus is the Client.

By our involvement during the early design phase, we are able to aid identifying the true, current and future needs of the facility in question.

Given our vast construction experience, we ensure the smooth transition from design to implementation, offering the most suitable technical and cost effective solution.

Our philosophy is to protect the client and offer advice ensuring the success of the floor construction and not just to investigate at the end, if the specifications are met.
