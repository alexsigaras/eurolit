Concrete mix design

Since we are investigating concrete floors it is obligatory to talk about concrete and its properties. The impact of a carefully devised mix design is crucial to the workability and characteristics of the mixture. But not all mix designs are suited for every need.

Eurolit considers concrete quality the key to success and taking into account local availability and climatic conditions is able to offer expert advice on concrete category selection, granulometric distribution of aggregates, appropriate water to cement ratio, superplasticizer selection and compatible retarding admixtures, mixing techniques to achieve desired slump on jobsite, superplasticizer and steel fiber dosing techniques.
