Steel Fiber Reinforced Concrete

Eurolit, talking advantage of constant development in construction equipment, steel fiber reinforcement and materials, offers high tolerance traditional or jointless industrial floors, suited for both Free and Defined movement facilities according to Concrete Society's TR34, DIN 18202, DIN 15185 and ASTM standards.

Magnesite Screed

Suited mostly for areas that are heavily damaged and have surface hardness issues.

These securely bonded, over an excising concrete sub base , typically 10mm to 35mm in thickness screeds, are uniform, inorganic, self leveling, jointless, odor free, conductive (according to DIN 51953) and without any surface admixtures. Their mechanical properties like surface hardness of 170 N/mm² (according to DIN 272), tensile strength of 11 N/mm² and compression strength of 50N/mm² (according to DIN 18560) surpasses even the highest standards.

Diamond Polished Concrete

Superfloor Terrazzo

Suited mostly for areas with flatness and/or esthetic issues. Eurolit uses diamond technology to deeply grind and polish the floor according to the client's needs without the use of any chemicals, dust and odor emission. During the process, aggregates are exposed revealing the natural beauty hidden inside concrete. The new floor profile is much smoother, crack free, does not produce any dust, retains its monolithic and its slip resistant properties, is immune to wheel marks and requires no cleaning agents to maintain its shiny appearance severely minimizing maintenance cost and time.

Up Grade

Suited for areas suffering from deeply penetrated dirt or poor esthetics, Eurolit uses diamond technology to finely grind and polish the floor without the use of any chemicals, dust and odor emission. During the process even accumulated dirt is removed and the surface appearance looks even better than when brand new.

The new floor profile retains its surface hardness and monolithic properties, does not produce any dust, retains its slip resistant properties, is immune to wheel marks and requires no cleaning agents to maintain its shiny appearance severely minimizing maintenance cost and time.

Construction Process

Eurolit follows a systematic construction process to ensure the highest quality industrial floors. Our approach combines modern techniques with proven methodologies for optimal results.

Pre-Construction Phase

• Site assessment and soil analysis
• Detailed planning and specification review
• Material procurement and quality verification
• Equipment mobilization and team preparation

Construction Phase

• Substrate preparation and leveling
• Reinforcement installation (steel fiber or mesh)
• Concrete placement and finishing
• Curing and quality control testing

Post-Construction

• Final inspection and documentation
• Flatness surveying and compliance verification
• Handover and maintenance guidelines
• Ongoing support and warranty service

Quality Control

Quality control is integral to every Eurolit construction project. We implement rigorous testing and monitoring procedures throughout the construction process.

Material Testing

• Concrete mix design verification
• Steel fiber distribution analysis
• Admixture compatibility testing
• Aggregate quality assessment

Construction Monitoring

• Real-time concrete temperature monitoring
• Slump and consistency testing
• Placement rate and timing control
• Environmental condition documentation

Final Verification

• Core strength testing
• Surface hardness measurement
• Flatness surveying (FM/FF values)
• Crack inspection and documentation

Construction Joints

Proper joint design and construction is critical for long-term floor performance. Eurolit specializes in advanced joint solutions for industrial facilities.

Joint Types

• Construction Joints: Planned breaks in concrete placement
• Control Joints: Controlled crack induction joints
• Isolation Joints: Separation from structural elements
• Expansion Joints: Movement accommodation joints

Joint Design Considerations

• Traffic loading and wheel loads
• Environmental temperature variations
• Subgrade conditions and movement
• Facility operational requirements

Installation & Sealing

• Precise cutting and forming techniques
• Load transfer device installation
• Appropriate sealant selection
• Long-term maintenance planning

Jointless Floors

Eurolit specializes in jointless industrial floors that eliminate maintenance issues associated with traditional joints while providing superior performance for high-traffic facilities.

Advantages of Jointless Design

• Reduced maintenance requirements
• Improved hygiene and cleanability
• Enhanced durability and longevity
• Superior ride quality for material handling equipment

Construction Methodology

• Steel fiber reinforced concrete systems
• Continuous placement techniques
• Advanced curing methods
• Post-tensioning applications where required

Applications

• High-bay VNA warehouses
• Food processing facilities
• Pharmaceutical manufacturing
• Clean room environments
