Construction joints

The area where the adjacent pours meet is subject to load transfers and wear from the operation of the trucks.

Construction joints pose a common problem of industrial floors, often derived from poor selection of joint profile characteristics such as load transfer capacity, dowel type, dimensions, profile spacing, finishing and poor installation

Eurolit is able to offer expert advice on joint profile properties, joint profile selection and installation methods taking into consideration the individual needs of each project regarding the characteristics that the profiles should meet to ensure the smooth operation of the facility.
